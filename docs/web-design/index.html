What's included

Everything you need to launch a serious business website.

Our website design plans bundle hosting, CMS, email, SEO and ongoing access — so you can focus on running your business while we handle the technology.

DNS Transfer

We point your domain to our secure servers and handle the DNS configuration so your launch is seamless and downtime is minimized.

WordPress Installation

WordPress content management system gives you the ability to update your website through a secured administrative login — no developer required for routine updates.

Database Set-up

All of our websites run on MySQL database which stores your website content and user information — configured for performance and reliability.

Email Set-up

Unlimited email accounts on your own domain (e.g., name@yourdomain.com), including spam filtering and webmail access.

Secured Administrative Control Panel

Once your website is on our servers you will have access to make all changes through an SSL-secured admin panel.

Web Hosting Control Panel

Check your website stats and traffic, set up new email addresses, and perform many other advanced functions — all from one place.

Unlimited Web Pages

We quote you on the initial pages we set up, but you can add an unlimited number of pages anytime through wp-admin.

Managed Hosting

Fast, secure, and monitored hosting included. Daily backups, SSL, and uptime monitoring out of the box.

On-Page SEO

Optimization for your chosen keywords — title tags, meta descriptions, H1 hierarchy, and image naming.

Search Engine Submissions

Social Media Links

Phone-First Lead Capture
